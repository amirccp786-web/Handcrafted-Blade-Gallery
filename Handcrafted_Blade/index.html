THE HAMON LINE

Our process respects the ancient traditions of metallurgy. The distinct 'Hamon' or temper line on our blades is not merely decorative; it is the physical manifestation of differential heat treatment.

By applying a specialized clay mixture to the spine of the blade before quenching, we ensure the edge achieves unparalleled hardness while the spine retains flexibility. This is the intersection of raw utility and refined artistry.
